FIRST NATIONAL BANK

FNB is the oldest bank in South Africa, and can be traced back to the Eastern Province Bank formed in Grahamstown in 1838. Today, FNB trades as a division of FirstRand Bank Limited. On 30 June 1999, the banking interests of FirstRand formally merged into a single entity to form FirstRand Bank.
